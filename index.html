Welcome to the Heart of Nehemia Ministry Kenya: A Beacon of Unwavering Faith, Transformative Hope, and Christ's Boundless Love for All People

Founded in 2006 by the visionary leadership of Bishop David Walukhu and Reverend Selina Walukhu, Nehemia Ministry Kenya stands as a vibrant testament to God's enduring love and power in Mukhonje, Kakamega County, and across the nation. We are a dynamic, spirit-filled community dedicated to holistic transformation – nurturing souls, empowering minds, healing bodies, and uplifting communities through the unwavering truth of the Gospel and tangible acts of compassion. Join us on this incredible journey of faith as we impact lives and extend God's kingdom.

Our Journey of Faith and Service

Since our humble beginnings in 2006, Nehemia Ministry Kenya has been on a remarkable journey, guided by God's grace and an unshakeable commitment to serve. What started as a small fellowship in Mukhonje, Kakamega County, under the heartfelt leadership of Bishop David and Reverend Selina Walukhu, has blossomed into a multi-faceted ministry touching thousands of lives. Our history is a tapestry woven with threads of faith, perseverance, community engagement, and countless answered prayers. We have expanded our reach not just in spiritual nourishment but also in providing essential services like healthcare through the Kingdom Faith-Based Hospital, vocational and biblical training via Matco Training Institute, and a loving home for vulnerable children at New Promise Orphanage. Our mission is to embody Christ's love in word and deed, fostering spiritual growth, empowering individuals to reach their God-given potential, and building resilient, hope-filled communities across Kenya and beyond. We are more than a church; we are a family, a movement, and a beacon of light, continually striving to make a lasting positive impact.

Our Core Ministries: Serving Humanity, Reflecting Christ

At the heart of Nehemia Ministry Kenya are our diverse and impactful ministries, each designed to address critical needs within our communities while reflecting the compassionate love of Jesus Christ. These are not just programs, but lifelines of hope, healing, and empowerment, driven by dedicated teams and the generous support of partners like you.

Kingdom Faith-Based Hospital

The Kingdom Faith-Based Hospital is a sanctuary of healing and hope, providing accessible, high-quality, and compassionate healthcare services to all, irrespective of their background. We integrate medical excellence with spiritual care, believing in holistic well-being. Our services range from general outpatient and inpatient care to specialized clinics, maternal and child health, and community health outreach programs, all delivered with a spirit of servitude and love.

Key Services:

  • General Medical & Surgical Care
  • Maternal & Child Health Services
  • Laboratory & Pharmaceutical Services
  • Spiritual Counseling & Support

Get Involved:

  • Pray for our patients and staff
  • Volunteer your medical expertise
  • Donate towards medical supplies
Explore Hospital Ministry

Matco Training Institute (Malaha)

Matco Training Institute, located in Malaha, is dedicated to empowering individuals with practical vocational skills and a strong foundation in biblical teachings. We offer diverse courses such as tailoring, hairdressing, carpentry, computer studies, and discipleship programs. Our goal is to equip students not only for sustainable livelihoods but also for impactful Christian leadership and service within their communities, fostering entrepreneurship and moral integrity.

Our Programs Include:

  • Vocational Skills Training (Tailoring, IT, etc.)
  • Biblical Studies & Discipleship
  • Leadership Development Workshops
  • Entrepreneurship & Business Skills

Support Our Students:

  • Sponsor a student's tuition
  • Donate learning materials/equipment
  • Mentor our aspiring entrepreneurs
Discover Matco Institute

New Promise Orphanage (Lwandeti)

The New Promise Orphanage in Lwandeti provides a safe, nurturing, and Christ-centered home for orphaned and vulnerable children. We strive to offer more than just shelter; we provide holistic care encompassing education, healthcare, spiritual guidance, and emotional support. Our aim is to raise these precious children to become responsible, God-fearing individuals with a bright future, knowing they are loved and valued members of God's family.

Our Care Focuses On:

  • Safe & Loving Residential Care
  • Nutritional Meals & Healthcare
  • Formal Education & Tutoring
  • Spiritual & Moral Development

Help Us Nurture Futures:

  • Sponsor a child's upbringing
  • Contribute to daily operational needs
  • Visit and share your love
