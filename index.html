Our Sacred Genesis: A Journey of Faith and Profound Impact

In the year of our Lord 2006, guided by the divine hand and the visionary leadership of Bishop David Walukhu alongside the compassionate stewardship of Reverend Selina Walukhu, Nehemia Ministry Kenya was birthed. From its humble beginnings in the fertile grounds of Mukhonje, nestled within Kakamega County, what started as a whispered prayer and a fervent dream has, by God's grace, blossomed into an expansive, vibrant beacon of hope, spiritual enlightenment, and tangible transformation that resonates throughout Kenya and beyond.

Our esteemed founders, imbued with an unshakeable conviction in the holistic and redemptive power of God's living Word, envisioned a ministry that transcends mere spiritual nourishment. They foresaw a dynamic entity actively engaging in the upliftment of individuals' physical, emotional, and social well-being, thereby reflecting the comprehensive love of Christ. This foundational philosophy has been the bedrock of our ministry, propelling us to extend our reach far beyond the sanctuary walls, directly into the heart of human need and societal challenges.

Nehemia Ministry is more than an institution; it is a living, breathing family, intricately woven together by a shared, sacred purpose: to cultivate resilient individuals, fortify families with divine principles, and foster thriving communities grounded in the timeless virtues of Christian faith. Each milestone achieved, every life touched by grace, and each answered prayer stands as a resounding testament to the boundless faithfulness of our God, the unwavering dedication of our founders, and the collective spirit of the entire Nehemia Ministry congregation. We invite you to journey with us as we continue to pen this extraordinary narrative of faith, hope, and enduring love.

Pillars of Our Faith: Our Unwavering Beliefs

The Holy Scriptures

We hold steadfastly that the Bible, encompassing the Old and New Testaments, is the divinely inspired, infallible, inerrant, and authoritative Word of God. It stands as the supreme and ultimate guide in all matters of faith, doctrine, and righteous conduct, illuminating our path and shaping our lives.

The Triune Godhead

We joyfully profess our belief in one true God, eternally co-existent in three distinct, yet inseparable persons: God the Father, Almighty Creator; God the Son, Jesus Christ, our Lord and Savior; and God the Holy Spirit, our Comforter and Guide. He is infinitely perfect, omnipotent, omniscient, and omnipresent, deserving of all glory, honor, and praise.

Salvation by Grace

We affirm that salvation is a magnificent, unmerited gift from God, graciously bestowed upon humanity through faith in Jesus Christ alone. His sacrificial death on the cross and glorious resurrection provide the sole means for the forgiveness of sins, reconciliation with God, and the promise of eternal life for all who repent and believe.

The Holy Spirit's Ministry

We believe fervently in the active, present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit. By His indwelling presence, believers are sanctified, empowered for godly living, and equipped with spiritual gifts for service. The Holy Spirit convicts the world of sin, righteousness, and judgment, and empowers the Church to fulfill Christ's Great Commission.

Our Branches of Compassion: Impactful Ministries

Kingdom Faith-Based Hospital

Extending God's healing touch by providing comprehensive, compassionate, and affordable healthcare services. Our hospital is a sanctuary where medical excellence meets spiritual solace, serving all with dignity and love, reflecting Christ's care for the sick and suffering.

Explore Our Healing Ministry

Matco Training Institute

Investing in the future by empowering youth and adults with transformative vocational skills, entrepreneurial knowledge, and Christ-centered character development at our Malaha campus. We are dedicated to unlocking potential and fostering self-sufficiency for a brighter tomorrow.

Discover Skill Empowerment

New Promise Orphanage

Providing a sanctuary of love, hope, and holistic care for orphaned and vulnerable children in Lwandeti. We are committed to nurturing their physical, emotional, educational, and spiritual growth, ensuring each child feels valued, safe, and empowered to reach their God-given potential.
